Privacy policy

We keep it simple.

HackTrader uses Google sign-in to identify you and maintain an authenticated session. We aim to collect the minimum information needed to let you into the product and keep the environment secure.

Information collection

We collect basic profile information provided by Google for authentication, such as your name and email address, strictly for sign-in and session management.

Data usage

Your data is used to identify you within the application, control access to protected pages, and maintain your authenticated dashboard session.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties for unrelated use. Authentication data is used internally to support access to the service.

Cookies and sessions

We use standard session cookies to keep you signed in while using the product. These are used for authentication continuity, not advertising personalization.
