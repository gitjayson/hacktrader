Market structure visualization

HackTrader

HackTrader is a market visualization tool for active traders. A correlation radar plots a focus ticker at the center and its peers on rings whose radius tracks live correlation strength — so a basket's alignment around an idea is something you can see at a glance instead of read out of a table. Support/resistance ladders, channel bands, and volume context fill in the rest of the structural picture. It is explicitly not a signal service: HackTrader describes what is currently visible in price, level, and correlation data, never what is about to happen.
